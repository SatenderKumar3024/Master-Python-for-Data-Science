Zomato Data Analysis Project - Satender Kumar with help of KPI data¶

Below is a short overview of the problem that the marketing team, along with me [Satender Kumar] (Data Analyst), wants to address to improve their new advertising plan and boost revenue in the future:¶

# 1) What type of restaurant do the majority of customers order from? # 2) How many votes has each type of restaurant received from customers? # 3) What are the ratings that the majority of restaurants have received? # 4) Zomato has observed that most couples order most of their food online. What is their average spending on each order? # 5) Which mode (online or offline) has received the maximum rating? # 6) Which type of restaurant received more offline orders, so that Zomato can provide those customers with some good offers? # 7) Predictive Analysis: Can we predict the rating of a restaurant based on its features such as type, number of votes, and cost? # 8) Sentiment Analysis: Analyze customer reviews (if available) to determine the sentiment and correlate it with the ratings. # 9) Trend Analysis: Identify any seasonal trends or patterns in customer orders and spending behavior. # 10) Customer Segmentation: Segment customers based on their ordering habits and preferences to target personalized marketing campaigns. # 11) Churn Prediction: Identify factors that might lead to customer churn and develop strategies to retain high-value customers. # 12) Price Elasticity: Analyze how changes in pricing might impact customer demand and restaurant ratings. # 13) Revenue Optimization: Suggest optimal pricing strategies for different restaurant types to maximize revenue. # 14) Location Analysis: If location data is available, analyze how the geographic location of restaurants affects online orders and ratings. # 15) Conversion Rate Analysis: Determine the conversion rate from views to orders for different restaurant types and identify ways to improve it.
